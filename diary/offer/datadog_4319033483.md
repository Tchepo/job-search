## Library Software Engineer - C++

Bordeaux, France; Grenoble, France; Lyon, France; Madrid, Spain; Montpellier, France; Nantes, France; Nice, France; Paris, France; Sophia Antipolis, France

**_At Datadog, we place value in our office culture - the relationships that it builds, the creativity it brings to the table, and the collaboration of being together. We operate as a hybrid workplace to ensure our employees can create a work-life harmony that best fits them._**

**What You’ll Do:** 

*   Develop and maintain high-performance C++ libraries that integrate with reverse proxies to enable observability and diagnostics
*   Design and implement runtime instrumentation for latency-sensitive and multi-threaded systems
*   Contribute to our adoption and integration of OpenTelemetry standards across Datadog’s C++ observability libraries
*   Own the performance, safety, and correctness of systems-level code running in production
*   Collaborate with open-source maintainers and engineering teams to improve tooling and ecosystem support
*   Investigate deep infrastructure bugs across boundaries (C++, proxies, OS, network stack)
*   Contribute to shaping our telemetry standards and cross-platform instrumentation strategies

**Who You Are:** 

*   You’ve written performant, production-grade C++ (C++14 or later) in complex systems
*   You’re confident with multithreading, memory management, and debugging low-level concurrency bugs
*   You’re comfortable owning critical paths in systems that need to be fast, observable, and safe under load
*   You communicate clearly and thrive in remote, distributed, or async-first teams

**Bonus points:**

*   You’ve worked on or contributed to open-source reverse proxy projects
*   You’ve built or maintained developer-facing libraries or SDKs
*   You have experience working on infrastructure components such as proxies, load balancers, or gateway layers
*   You’re familiar with system-level observability tools (perf, eBPF, systemtap, etc.)
*   You’ve debugged performance bottlenecks in HTTP, TLS, or TCP/IP stacks
*   You have exposure to distributed systems and service meshes at scale

_Datadog values people from all walks of life. We understand not everyone will meet all the above qualifications on day one. That's okay. If you’re passionate about technology and want to grow your skills, we encourage you to apply._
