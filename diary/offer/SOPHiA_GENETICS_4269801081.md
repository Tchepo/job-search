## About the job

Are you an experienced Software Engineer with a passion for building and improving scalable, high-performance systems? Are you looking for a team that embraces fresh ideas, encourages an agile approach, and ensures your voice is heard and your contributions truly make an impact?  
  
If this sounds like you and you are driven by purpose, join the SOPHiA GENETICS Software Engineering team as our new **Senior Software Engineer**, and enable us to make a positive impact on the outcomes for cancer and rare disease patients worldwide.  
  
_This is a hybrid opportunity, requiring 3 days onsite our corporate office in either Rolle, CH or Bidart, FR.  
  
_**Our Mission  
  
**We believe there is a smarter, more data-driven way to make decisions in healthcare and our cloud-native AI powered SOPHiA DDM Platform makes that vision a reality on a daily basis. You will have direct input to our mission to democratize data-driven medicine for the ultimate benefit of cancer and rare disease patients across the globe.  
  
**Your Mission  
  
**As our **Senior Software Engineer**, you will be responsible for developing, optimizing, and continuously improving core systems, while contributing to technical initiatives and ensuring solutions meet both business and technical requirements. The components you'll work on and own are essential to SOPHiA GENETICS’ DDM operations, enabling the processing of medical data into insights for oncologists. These components span the technology stack and enable AI algorithims processing medical data to have a real-world impact via a robust and scalable architecture. Your expertise will ensure these components not only scale effectively but also remain efficient in terms of performance and cost, while maintaining overall system health.

**Requirements  
  
**

*   Solid proficiency in Java for backend services and React.js for front end development skills.
*   Solid understanding of microservices architecture.
*   Experience in optimization of system performance, manage resources effectively, and improve execution time- esp when dealing with VERY large data volumes.
*   Proficiency with databases like MySQL, MariaDB, and DuckDB, including design and query optimization.
*   Familiarity with Linux environments and GitLab for version control and CI/CD pipelines.
*   Experience with scalable architecture, particularly in cloud environments and containerized infrastructure.
*   Experience in Micronaut for developing microservices and backend solutions is a strong plus.
*   Familiarity with monitoring and observability tools like Grafana and Loki.
*   Knowledge of Kafka is a plus
*   Experience in genomics is beneficial.
